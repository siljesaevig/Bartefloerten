3. Purpose, goals and audience

The purpose of this webside is to help single people with dating, flirting and finding "the right one". Our side will have tips for him and her. There will also be advice for couples who wants to keep the sparkle in their relationship. We wish to inspire our users to find a partner and keeping him/her. If you have trouble with the whole dating thing and have many questions this is the side for you. On this webside we will respond to the users question and give advices. Our goal is to me our users a little bit more comfortable with flirting and dating. Our users will mainly be boys and girls who are looking for love. We want to make it easyer and help them on their way to find love. Couples who wants to keep the spark in their relationship could also use this webside. So mainly anybody can visit this webpage and find something useful.
